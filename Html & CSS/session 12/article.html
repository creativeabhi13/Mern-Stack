Bumrah Bowling

Jasprit Bumrah is an Indian cricketer who plays for the Indian national cricket team in all formats of the game. He is a right-arm fast bowler. He made his debut in One Day Internationals on 23 January 2016 against Australia and in T20Is on 26 January 2016 against Australia. He is the third Indian to take a hat-trick in Test cricket.

Hardik Pandya Batting

Hardik Himanshu Pandya is an Indian international cricketer who plays for Baroda in domestic cricket and Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League. He is an all-rounder who bats right-handed and bowls right-arm fast-medium. He is the younger brother of Krunal Pandya.

Virat Kohli Batting

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the India national team. A right-handed top-order batsman, Kohli is regarded as one of the best contemporary batsmen in the world. He plays for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League, and has been the team's captain since 2013.
